Friends I Made Through Gaming


by Sayle Makdessi

291Poem


Generated code that brings us pleasure

Accompanied by domains lasting forever:

Magic Circles that transport us elsewhere

Emerged newly from pieces of hardware.


Traversing worlds as we command

Exploring plot as it was planned.

Changing the medium for better or for worse

Hatred opens the Gate which gamers traverse.

New play in a world that’s virtual

Offering an alternative to the physical,

Losing yourself, too focused, too immersed

Overflowing sensations, addicted at worst.

Gaming your worries away

You press start to play.


by Marwan Bassam

A World In My World


Back in the day when i was a young child

The Nintendo 64 instantly had me wired

A plastic device with a game in the shape of a block

Super Mario 64 had me jumping on shells and eating mushrooms a lot

The simplicity was key, never did I think I’d need more

Until game developers decided to create a new door

Fast forward to 2021

The way video-gaming has now become

can be looked at as a lifestyle rather than just for fun

A new world to dive into

A different way to interact with people

Now considered an E-Sport

A way to make money that is in fact legal

It blows my mind to see careers be made out of a fake reality

But the passion for a universe that is stuck in your screen could be our originality

From so basic to now so in depth

Even Pokemon has become more than just a bunch of texts

Open-world games with cities to explore

and High skill games for players to compete

Story-lines waiting for you to absorb

and tournaments to win a treat

The addiction is real

The grind has changed

Instead of playing on my own

I have my friends online to engage

It has become a reality in my reality, a world in my world

and to my surprise, there is still more to unfold.


by Daniel Daher
